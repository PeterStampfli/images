Two mirrors

Two mirror lines make a dihedral group with k-fold rotational symmetry if they meet at an angle of 180o/k.

k:

We map any point of the output image into the sector between the mirror lines with an element of the group.

This is much faster than using a chain of several mirror images within a ray-tracing approach.

The input image is like a skin. Maybe beautyful, but hiding the inner structure.
